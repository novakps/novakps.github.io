Canon Sure Shot Owl

About

The Canon Sure Shot Owl is a compact 35mm point and shoot camera known for its reliability and ease of use.

Specifications

  • Type: 35mm compact camera
  • Lens: 35mm f/3.5
  • Focus: Autofocus
  • Flash: Built-in
  • Year: 1994

How to Use

Basic instructions for using the Canon Sure Shot Owl:

  1. Load the film by opening the back cover
  2. Insert the film cartridge and pull the film leader to the orange mark
  3. Close the back cover, and the camera will automatically advance the film
  4. Turn on the camera by sliding the lens cover open
  5. Look through the viewfinder and frame your shot
  6. Press the shutter button halfway to focus
  7. Press the shutter button fully to take the picture

Features

The Canon Sure Shot Owl includes several useful features:

  • Auto-focus with focus lock
  • Built-in flash with red-eye reduction
  • Self-timer
  • Date imprinting (on some models)
  • Automatic film loading and rewinding
