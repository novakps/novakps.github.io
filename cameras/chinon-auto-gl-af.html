Chinon Auto GL AF

About

The Chinon Auto GL AF is a compact 35mm autofocus camera with a quality lens and versatile features.

Specifications

  • Type: 35mm compact camera
  • Lens: 38mm f/2.8
  • Focus: Autofocus
  • Exposure: Programmed automatic
  • Year: 1988

How to Use

Basic instructions for using the Chinon Auto GL AF:

  1. Load the film by opening the back cover
  2. Insert the film cartridge and pull the film leader to the mark
  3. Close the back cover, and the camera will automatically advance the film
  4. Turn on the camera
  5. Look through the viewfinder and frame your shot
  6. Press the shutter button halfway to focus
  7. Press the shutter button fully to take the picture

Features

The Chinon Auto GL AF includes several useful features:

  • Auto-focus system
  • Built-in flash
  • Self-timer
  • Automatic film loading and rewinding
  • DX film speed setting
