Minolta Talker

About

GarboMinolta Speaks! The Minolta Talker is a unique camera that provides voice feedback.

Specifications

  • Type: 35mm compact camera with voice feedback
  • Lens: 35mm f/2.8
  • Focus: Autofocus
  • Year: 1984

How to Use

Basic instructions for using the Minolta Talker:

  1. Load the film
  2. Turn on the camera
  3. Listen for voice prompts
  4. Frame your shot
  5. Press the shutter button

Voice Commands

The Minolta Talker provides voice feedback for various situations:

  • "Too dark, use flash"
  • "Load film"
  • "Check distance"
  • "Too close"
